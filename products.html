Recommended equipment

Dessert cups

I use dessert cups to display my desserts, here are some affordable ones that I recommend. I usually use the cuboid or dynasty cups for milkcakes and the rainbow, cube and angular are my absolute favs for mini desserts like cheesecake!

Milkcake Pipettes

These pipettes hold a small amount of milkcake sauce and it's not very functional but so aesthetically pleasing!

Piping Nozzles

I love the Wilton piping nozzles, they're affordable and easy to use. I purchase mine from Hobbycraft but Amazon and others stock them too.

Piping Bags

Any piping bags work, I use these from Amazon.

Stand Mixer/ Hand Whisk

The Kitchenaid stand mixer is a staple in the baking world but I know it can be expensive so I've also linked a more affordable one that works for all of your mixing needs!
